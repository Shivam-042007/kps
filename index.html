About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence since 1996

The journey of **Kerala Public School, Rairangpur** began in 1996 when the bricks of its foundation were laid under the guardianship of the **KPS Trust**. Born from the "Missionary Zeal" of our founders, the school was established to fulfill the long-cherished wish of the people of Bamanghati Subdivision for a quality English Medium School.

Starting with just 55 students, today we are affiliated with CISCE (New Delhi) and continue to mold students into responsible citizens, prioritizing discipline and character over rote learning.

  • CISCE Affiliated Curriculum
  • Value-Based Education
  • Focus on Holistic Development
